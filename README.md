# Hotels_Analysis
Exploratory Data Analytics in Hotels Analysis

We have an imaginary company called AtliQ Grands in this project. This is a hotel chain which operates in various cities in India and they have hotels in Delhi, Mumbai, Hyderabad, Benguluru.
They had been in industry for 20 years

They have hotels like: AtliQ Seasons, AtliQ Exotica, AtliQ Bay and AtliQ Palace
Types of rooms: standard, Elite, Premium and Presidential

A person can book hotels via various medium : AtliQ Grands own website, 3P booking and Offline booking

This is lossing its revenue, market share with its competitors so here we are trying to figure out and exploring data to find out the reason.
